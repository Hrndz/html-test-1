Top 10 Scarie Movies

#10

Insidious (La noche del demonio)

This is a saga of 4 horror movies.
A family looks to prevent evil spirits from trapping their comatose child in a realm called The Further.

#9

Annabelle

Another great saga.
Determined to keep Annabelle from wreaking more havoc, Ed and Lorraine Warren bring the possessed doll to the locked artifacts room in their home.

#8

The Conjuring

A family calls on supernatural investigators Ed & Lorraine Warren for help against a terrifying evil in their house.

#7

IT

Six childhood friends reunite in their small hometown in Maine to once again confront an evil entity they thought they had destroyed 30 years earlier.

#6

Hereditary

After the death of her mentally ill mother, a woman and her family uncover their evil lineage.

#5

Talk to me

When Mia and her friends discover how to conjure spirits using an embalmed hand,
they become hooked on the new thrill until it goes too far and unleashes terrifying supernatural forces.
Now, they must choose who to trust: the living or the dead.

#4

Terrifier

Art The Clown returns and sets his sights on three young women, along with anyone else that gets in his way.

#3

Jigsaw

A mysterious madman rounds up a group of victims to play sadistic games of life and death.
Police soon find evidence that link the crimes to Jigsaw, the infamous killer who died 10 years earlier.

#2

The Exorcist

A modern reinvention inspired by William Peter Blatty's original 1971 book, THE EXORCIST is a propulsive, serialized, psychological thriller.

#1

The Nun

The sequel to the worldwide smash hit follows Sister Irene as she once again comes face to face with Valak, the demon nun.
